About the Technology

Salesforce is the world’s #1 customer relationship management (CRM) platform. It empowers industries to manage their day to day customer and sales channel. Their software and applications are focused on sales, customer service, marketing automation, analytics, and application development.

About the Role

A Salesforce Administrator works with stakeholders to define system requirements and customize the platform. To put it simply, they enable users to get the most out of Salesforce technology. A Salesforce Admin best understands how to make the platform work for their company’s goals.
